AUTOMOTIVE OILS

HP Lubricants engages in the production and distribution of an extensive spectrum of lubricants designed to address diverse automotive applications comprehensively. Employing rigorous quality oversight, resilient manufacturing methodologies, and a proficient team of Technical Services Engineers, HP Lubricants diligently satisfies every customer's needs.

Evident in the creation of widely recognized brand names like HP Milcy, HP Racer, and the HP Neosynth range of oils, HP Lubricants has cultivated a strong presence in households. Furthermore, strategic collaborations with multiple Auto Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs) enable the marketing of co-branded and Genuine Oils, all of which are approved by the respective OEMs.
