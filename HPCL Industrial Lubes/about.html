About Us

Alcon & Co. was established by its Proprietor Mr. Atul Mukhi in 1999. Soon he was joined by his wife, Mrs. Parul Mukhi, and PM Sales was established to expand the Business further in more Industrial Areas. Since then, our proprietor-owned business has been built on a foundation of trust with our valued customers. For over two decades, we have proudly served our community, offering unparalleled quality and service. Our commitment to excellence has garnered us a loyal customer base who rely on us for their needs. Our unwavering dedication to customer satisfaction sets us apart. As we look to the future, we remain steadfast in our mission to uphold the trust and confidence our customers have placed in us since day one.
