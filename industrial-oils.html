Industrial Oils

Offering an extensive array of Industrial Oils, HP Lubricants effectively caters to a diverse spectrum of industries. Fueled by inherent advantages, HP Lubricants occupies the forefront of the industrial oil market share in India. Esteemed by our clients for unwavering quality, punctual deliveries, and robust technical support, HP Lubricants sustains its industry growth trajectory through the introduction of progressively advanced products, aligned with evolving requirements

In applications spanning hydraulic systems, enclosed gear boxes, compressors, pumps, pneumatic tools, and general purpose equipment, HP's lubricant brands like Enklo, Parthan, Hycom, Numatic, Yantrol, and more play a pivotal role. As a result, HP Lubricants consistently strengthens its position in the industry while meeting the diverse and evolving needs of clients
